MindBricks ¬∑ Flow Summary

1) Start a focus session

Pomodoro runs and records light, noise, phone pickups.

2) 10-second check-in

Quick questionnaire captures perceived productivity (ground truth).

3) Motivate

Earn coins to build your house; ending early removes progress ‚Äî gentle pressure.

4) Learn on-device

Update the user's focus profile (habits + environment) privately on the phone.

5) Plan tomorrow

Generate a color-coded timeline: best hours + suggested environments.

6) Repeat daily

More data ‚Üí better model ‚Üí improved focus plan and rewards.
